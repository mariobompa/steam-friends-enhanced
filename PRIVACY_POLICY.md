# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 1, 2026

## Overview
Steam Friends Manager is committed to protecting your privacy. This extension is designed to work entirely locally on your device without sending data to external servers.

## Data Collection & Storage
- **All data is stored locally** using Chrome's `storage.sync` API
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No telemetry or analytics are collected
- No personal information is shared with third parties

## Data We Store
The extension stores the following information on your device:
- Friend notes (text you add manually)
- Custom group names (that you create)
- Friend-to-group assignments (which friends are in which groups)

This data is associated with your Steam Community account in your local Chrome storage.

## Permissions
- **storage** - Required to save your notes and groups locally
- **https://steamcommunity.com** - Required to access the Steam Community friends page

## Your Data Rights
- All data belongs to you
- You can delete all data by uninstalling the extension
- You can export your data by accessing Chrome's storage settings
- No data is retained after uninstallation

## Disclaimer
This extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or associated with Valve Corporation or Steam.

## Questions?
If you have any privacy concerns, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
